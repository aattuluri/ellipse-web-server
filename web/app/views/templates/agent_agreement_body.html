SCHEDULE A

RESPONSIBILITIES, COSTS, TERM OF ENGAGEMENT, AND PAYMENT

RESPONSIBILITIES:

The Merchant will use the Agent Avery online platform’s merchant dashboard (also called “travel agent dashboard” or “agent dashboard”) to receive prospective client leads (“Leads”) and interact with the clients via the same. It will endeavor to address any inquiries made by the users of Agent Avery within twenty-four (24) hours. Any Merchant not responding to a Lead provided by Agent Avery within 24 hours will forfeit its opportunity to service that Lead and the said Lead will be sent to another Merchant.

Merchant shall not make any promises or guarantees it may not be able to fulfil, such as, but not limited to, being able to book a trip to a destination it has no special knowledge of or claiming to assemble a full itinerary within a timeframe that it cannot realistically meet.

It is the sole responsibility of the Merchant to determine the quality of the Lead and determine whether and when to charge an upfront service fee in the form of a deposit towards a final purchase. The Merchant may never charge a service fee to book the traveler’s trip, unless it is giving a general recommendation about the trip and assembling an itinerary without making the actual bookings on behalf of the user.

COSTS:

Monthly fee: The Merchant will access the Agent Avery merchant dashboard for a fee of $12.99 a month or any other fee as advertised on the Agent Avery’s website and chosen by the Merchant. This fee will be charged on a monthly basis unless cancelled by the Merchant in writing. There shall be no refunds for cancellations occurring part way through the billing cycle.

Lead: In addition, the Merchant will pay a nominal fee to purchase Leads from Agent Avery. The cost of Leads may depend on the potential transaction value of the trip, demand for the Lead among other Merchants, and other factors determined by the Company at its sole discretion.

Commission on Total Transaction Value (TTV): Moreover, the Company shall charge a commission on the TTV of successfully booked trips according to the schedule below:

Commission Tier Structure

Commission on TTV

$4,000 to $6,999.99

0.25%

$7,000 to $9,999.99

0.35%

Greater than or equal to $10,000

0.50%, limit of $98

Example: Commission on trip worth $5,000 would be $12.50.

The Company will not charge a commission on trips that are not successfully booked. In the event that the trip is cancelled, commission may still be assessed if the Merchant has received a commission from its supplier or host agency.

Commission on Service Fee: If the Merchant is unable to make travel bookings on behalf of the user due to limitations of its license or for other reasons elects to provide travel assistance only in the form travel advice in return for a flat service fee, and does not make bookings on behalf of the user, Agent Avery shall take a commission of 25% on such service fee.

Example: Merchant charges a service fee of $50 to the user plan a three day itinerary. Agent Avery takes a commission of $12.50.

 

TERM OF ENGAGEMENT:

This engagement shall commence upon execution of this Agreement and shall continue in full force and effect unless terminated earlier by operation of and in accordance with this Agreement. Inactivity on the part of the Merchant greater than twelve (12) months may cause the Merchant account to become disabled.

COMPENSATION:

The Company is not the merchant-of-record for trips planned by the Merchant. The Merchant is solely responsible for collecting payment details from the user to make purchases on behalf of the user and/or charge any service fees by sending a link to its payment collection page through the Agent Avery online platform. The Company is not responsible or shall be held liable for any data breach or loss related to payment collection or transactions. It is the Merchant’s responsibility to ensure safekeeping of the user’s payment information that it collects to make purchases on behalf of the users. The Company is not a part of any purchase agreement made between the user and the Merchant.
